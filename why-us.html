Why Us

Over the past decades, Patil Technologies has successfully completed several structures across Western India. Accuracy, precision, timely completion and excellent service with a smile are the virtues we live by. This is what has made us cater to a diverse clientele ranging from large PSU’s to private properties and from hospitals to stadiums.

Some of our valued customers:

NTPC, Solapur

Symbiosis Universities, Pune

RIECO & Thermax Industries, Pune

Ghodawat Institutes, Atigre (Kolhapur)

TCL Cummins, Lonand (Phaltan)

KLE Education Society, Belgavi  

In this context, Patil Technologies has also embarked on the journey to excellence along with its ever existent motto of Total Customer Satisfaction. We now present our own designed Stadium Gallerias, Parking Lots, Malls and even Aircraft Hangars. This helps our clients in achieving utmost convenience as well as build their image with futuristic aesthetics.
